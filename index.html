Three things take away sadness: water, greenery and a smiling face

The greenery, no matter if it is a lawn in an open plaza, a greenway in a peaceful town, a parterre in a busy street, or a tree in a small courtyard, has power to give people a chance to breath the nature; they can feel the nature while watching trees changing colors, smelling fragrance from flowers or sitting on the lawn and touch the moist dirt. In comfortable green-land, people will be more likely to perform actively and communicate with others, through which a lively urban space will be created.

Project Information

Located in the metropolitan development circles, Xincheng town has convenient transportation, as well as a long history with rich natural resources. In the Comprehensive Renovation of Small Towns in 2017, many polluting factories have been closed, which has improved the environment a lot. However, with the decline of the relevant industry, a large number of workers are leaving the town, making the already depressed economy even worse.

Town Development Timeline

INCEPTION

(Before 841 AC.)

Primitive ancesters were fishing and hunting in this place tracing back to 5000 BC. In 500 BC., Fu Chai, King of Wu built rampart here to defense Kingdom Yue. In 841 AC., the town was first established as a market town named Shilin

PROSPERITY

(1200-1750)

Dring the prosperity in southern china from 1200-1573 AC, the town witnessed the booming of sericulture and agriculture. The foundation layout of the current town was formed before 1750.

INDUSTRIALIZATION

(1949-2000)

The Xincheng Town was established as town of Jiaxing City in 1949. Lots of factories was constructed in the 1970s.

MODERIZATION

(2000-present)

Masterplan of Jiaxing City published in 2006 has forward the urbanization. In 2017, the ”low-end, small, scattered” factories were closed. Policy of “Village relocation and Renovation of small towns” was carried out to improve the environment

Town Land Use Planning

The master plan (by the year of 2035) orientates the town to develop high-tech industry, cultural tourism, and ecological regimens. In order to achieve economic growth and improve the residents' living conditions, new industries will be introduced and the infrastructure systems will be improved. Additionally, the ecological system and historical neighborhood will be preserved.
